Explore Kerala

Kerala, a state situated on the tropical Malabar Coast of southwestern India, is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the country. Named as one of the ten paradises of the world by National Geographic Traveler, Kerala is famous especially for its ecotourism initiatives and beautiful backwaters.

Food

Kerala cuisine is a culinary style originated in the Kerala, a region in the south of India. Kerala cuisine offers a multitude of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes prepared using fish, poultry and red meat with rice a typical accompaniment.

Festivals

The festival season, the pride and joy of Kerala, is epitomised by the ferocious beats of the chenda emanating across the landscape. Our festivals celebrate the traditions passed down by our ancestors.Be it Onam,Christmas, all of Kerala rejoices with one spirit to bring the entire coastal State to life.
